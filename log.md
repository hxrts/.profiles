### Regex

#### match from 'somestring' to the end of the line

(somestring.*)$

#### match from the beginning of the line to 'somestring'

.*(somestring)

#### match between two strings

(stringone)(.*)(stringtwo)		# between strings
(?<=stringone)(.*)(?=stringtwo)		# including those strings
